# Security Policy

FDCxCapSense is an Arduino sensor-library project and does not provide safety-critical decisions.

## Reporting

Please do not publish sensitive reports before maintainers have reviewed them. Open a private advisory if the hosting platform supports it, or contact the maintainer listed in `library.properties`.

## Scope

Security-relevant issues may include:

- unsafe blocking behavior,
- buffer/serialization issues,
- incorrect validation that could mislead users,
- claim wording that implies safety use.

Hardware validation and field behavior are owner-side responsibilities.
