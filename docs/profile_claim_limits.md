# Profile Claim Limits

All application profiles are experimental.

They provide:

- raw metrics
- compensated metrics
- diagnostic flags
- confidence values
- short technical reasons
- candidate or proxy states

They do not provide final safety, field-validated, or product-grade decisions.

The profile outputs depend on electrode geometry, shielding, grounding, cable length, environment, temperature, humidity, material properties, calibration workflow, and independent validation.

Do not connect profile outputs directly to actuators or safety decisions without a separate validated system design.
